Minutes played


The total number of minutes played across the season.

Shot conversion efficiency


Shot conversion is the ratio of goals scored against how many shots occurred. The higher this number, the more efficiently the player converted shots into goals.

Assist efficiency


A key pass is a pass which resulted in a goal-scoring opportunity which was then missed. Using this and the assisted goals, we can calculate how effective a player's goal-creating passing is.



Goals and xG


xG is a metric which is calculated to reflect how many goals you might expect a player to score on average given the quality of opportunities to score that they had during a match. The line shows xG whereas the bars show the goals scored.

Assists and xA

Similarly to xG, xA is a measure of an expected output you might expect on average from a player during a game, but this time for assists rather than goals. The line shows xA overlayed with the number of assists per game.



Effect on league position

This plot shows the effect that the goals and assists had on the league position at the end of the season.

Defensive xG effect per 90


This shows how well a player's team defends when the player is in the side. It is the sum of the differences between the opponent xG and the average xG for that opponent across the season, averaged out across all the games that player played in. A number above 0 means that the team were on average better at defending. A number equal to 1 would mean that the team conceeded on average 1 xG fewer per game across the season that the league average.
